📋 NICE HTE19 — mandatory evidence generation obligations

All commissioners deploying NICE HTE19-recommended COPD apps must have evidence generation agreements with NICE and submit annual reports. This is a condition of the recommendation — not optional. See the NICE HTE19 evidence generation entry below.

NICE HTE19 Evidence Generation Programme

NICE

Open

All four NICE HTE19 COPD apps are in a 3-year evidence generation period. Commissioning organisations are expected to support evidence collection through annual reporting agreements with NICE. This is a condition of the HTE19 recommendation, not a grant funding opportunity.

📅 3-year evidence generation period ends approximately December 2027.
