Clinical safety: This product has strict clinical inclusion/exclusion criteria and must only be deployed within a carefully designed pathway. Do not deploy without clinical safety review and specialist eating disorder service involvement.

Why it matters locally

Eating disorder services face significant capacity pressures. Overcoming Bulimia Online offers a structured digital CBT-based self-help programme that can be deployed within clear clinical boundaries for appropriate patients — reducing waiting times and expanding access.
