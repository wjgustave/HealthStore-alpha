Why it matters locally

Oviva combines app-based food tracking, goal-setting and personalised dietitian support to deliver weight management at scale. It is particularly relevant to commissioners building pathways for patients on anti-obesity medications (GLP-1 agonists) who need structured behavioural support.

Clinical evidence

Full evidence record from the HTE19 dossier. Links to source publications provided where available.

NICE HTG700 recommendation. Multiple peer-reviewed studies supporting app-delivered dietitian-supported weight management. Evidence includes RCTs and real-world studies across NHS and international settings.
