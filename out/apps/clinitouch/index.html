Why it matters locally

Clinitouch enables NHS organisations to stand up virtual COPD wards that reduce unplanned admissions and free up bed capacity. It supports 24/7 monitoring hub models and integrates with HL7 FHIR. Spirit Health is explicitly named in NHSE winter 2024 COPD technology funding.

Clinical evidence

Full evidence record from the HTE19 dossier. Links to source publications provided where available.

Two peer-reviewed observational studies (Ghosh et al. 2016, 2018) showing 67.5% reduction in unscheduled admissions and 484% ROI, a peer-reviewed economic evaluation of a COVID-19 digital ward (Swift et al. JMIR 2024) and an NHSX grey-literature case study. All key authors have declared Spirit Health affiliations. No independent RCT for Clinitouch in COPD has been identified. NICE HTE19 reviewed and recommended with conditions.

Ghosh S et al. · British Journal of Healthcare Management · 2016 · 22(3):123–131
DOI ↗
DOI: 10.12968/bjhc.2016.22.3.123Setting: Leicestershire Partnership NHS Trust — COPD in urban setting, 12 months

Key results: Admissions fell from 3.13 to 1.02 per patient (p<0.001). 67.5% reduction in unscheduled admissions. Net saving £622k from £52k service cost. £2,278 saving per patient.

Limitation: Observational. Combined intervention (Clinitouch + clinical health coaching + specialist nurse) — outcomes reflect the full model, not technology alone.

⚠ Commissioner note: Authors have Spirit Health affiliations with declared conflicts of interest. No independent RCT identified.

Clinitouch is explicitly named as eligible for NHSE ICB £125k COPD technology funding (August 2024 deadline). Spirit Health can support ICB applications — contact clinitouch@spirit-health.com.
Expected benefit
67.5% reduction in unscheduled COPD admissions in Leicester programme. 1,103 bed days saved in LLR ICS virtual ward. ROI evidence exists but carries conflict-of-interest caveat.
Tariff considerations
No live tariff calculator is available. Leicester programme generated £622k net saving from £52k service cost but this reflects a combined clinical and digital intervention. Consider the full service wrap cost when modelling local ROI.
ROI notes
484% system ROI cited (Ghosh 2018) — note declared COI. Spirit Health estimates ~194,000 bed days potential saving across England. Treat as directional rather than guaranteed.

Implementation
Model: Co-design with clinical teams to configure pathway parameters, questionnaire sets and alert thresholds before deployment. Community/ward staff support patient initial setup.
Monitoring: Active remote management model — clinical team or contracted 24/7 hub must review dashboard. This is not a self-management-only tool.
Hours caveat: Virtual ward model requires staffed monitoring capability. Confirm monitoring hours and escalation protocols with Spirit Health before deployment.
